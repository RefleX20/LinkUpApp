What LinkUp does

Everything your
group needs

One app for events, chat, and RSVPs. No group-chat chaos. No missed plans.

Group Events

Create events with dates, locations, and details — including repeating and multi-day events. RSVP Going, Maybe, or Can't Go, with capacity limits and RSVP deadlines.

Real-Time Group Chat

Messaging with reactions, replies, polls, and event tags — one thread per group, plus private direct messages.

Unified Calendar

See group plans alongside your personal calendar so you never double-book. Your personal events stay on your device — they're never uploaded.

Join in Seconds

Join a group by scanning a QR code or typing a 6-character code. Private groups require admin approval before anyone gets in.

Notifications That Respect You

New events, RSVPs, and reminders — with per-category controls and per-group muting, so you only hear about what you care about.

Private by Default

Event locations are stored encrypted on our servers, sign-in credentials live in the iOS Keychain, ads are non-personalized, and your data is never sold.

How it works

Three steps to
organized plans

Built for friend groups, clubs, teams, and families — anyone who plans things together.

1

Create or join a group

Start a group and share its code, or join one by scanning a QR code. Everyone lands in the same space.

2

Post an event

Pick a date, drop a pin or type an address, add the details. The whole group is notified instantly.

3

RSVP and chat

Everyone responds with one tap, chats in the group thread, and gets reminders before it starts.
